Feeling Hungry? Try these authenic Canadian restaurants.

Although many of the largest restaurant chains in Canada are US-based, some Canadian-based restaurant chains are growing and have expanded into other market, especially into the USA. To the left is a video on how to make Classic Canadian Poutine. Below are some popular Canadian chains.

Tim Hortons

Canadian classic. You can't drive a kilometer without passing one of these. Based in Toronto, Tim Hortons serves coffee, doughnuts, and other fast food items. It is Canada's largest quick-service restaurant chain, with 4,949 restaurants in 15 countries.

Boston Pizza

Take a break from cooking tonight and bring the kids to BP! Also great for sports teams and watching hockey. Boston Pizza, also known as Boston's The Gourmet Pizza Restaurant and Sports Bar outside of Canada, is a Canadian multinational restaurant chain that owns and franchises locations in Canada, the United States and Mexico.
