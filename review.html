Hosts Review

GoDaddy.com

  • GoDaddy is a great choice as you can create the biggest, or smallest sites with this host.
  • With a 2-year purchase you can get your first year for only $0.01!
  • GoDaddy is the global leader in domains offering more than 84 million domains.
  • Domains can be as cheap as a penny, and steadily increase depending on what you're looking for.
  • GoDaddy offers the lowest prices on the internet when choosing a provider, which makes it the perfect host for new businesses or company's that are starting out.

Fatcow.com

  • FatCow is a popular host that offers a lot for a small price!
  • You can use this website for only $4.08 per month.
  • FatCow offers 371 different domains to choose from. The more the merrier.
  • Along with your choice of domain and a userfriendly work environment, FatCow also offers plenty of storage, site templates, unlimted mailboxes and more.

HostGator.com

  • HostGator is yet another popular web hosting service that "scales from easy to expert".
  • Starting at only $2.75/month you can get started quickly and get free domain for a year.
  • HostGator currently hosts over 2 million websites which proves its security.
  • Mainstream domains such as ".com" ".net" and ".org" start at $12.95/year, however others can be as cheap as $0.95/year.
  • HostGator offers a variety of web hosting plans including shared hosting, website building, WordPress hosting, and VPS hosting.

After reviewing three different web hosts I believe that GoDaddy is the best option. It comes at an affordable price, and is easy to use. For a new business that is starting out, or a preexisting one, GoDaddy offers a variety of domains, and is widly used.
